Multi-Echelon Inventory Optimization

Solves the systematic safety-stock misallocation problem endemic to retailers: over-stocking at stores, under-stocking at DCs — caused by optimizing each echelon independently. Clark-Scarf decomposition propagates service-level constraints bottom-up across a 3-tier network (CDC → Regional WH → Store), with risk pooling quantified explicitly.

📉
15–30% safety stock reduction at equivalent or better service levels vs. independent single-echelon policies.

Supply Network Design Optimization

Capacitated Facility Location Problem (CFLP) solved as a Mixed-Integer Program across a Central European distribution network. Determines which warehouses to open and how to assign customer demand zones — minimizing the sum of fixed facility costs, transportation costs, and handling costs simultaneously.

🏭
5 warehouses selected from 20 candidates: Łódź, Berlin, Katowice, Vienna, Budapest — covering 50 customer zones across 6 countries.

Fixed/Variable Cost Crossover

MIP finds the exact point where marginal fixed cost of opening a warehouse equals marginal transport savings. At ×1.25 fixed costs, optimal WH count shifts from 5 → 4.

🔗
Capacity as Topology Driver

The 6th warehouse is triggered by capacity constraint binding at 150% demand — not transport cost. No heuristic detects this without explicit capacity modeling.

📐
Greedy ≠ Optimal

Greedy heuristic centralizes to single cheapest hub. MIP reveals 5 distributed nodes is cheaper when transport savings outweigh fixed cost additions by 5–15%.

Multi-Period Production Planning Optimization

Capacitated Lot Sizing Problem (CLSP) solved as a 4-layer optimization system. Generates a week-by-week production schedule for 8 products across 3 shared production lines, incorporating promotional demand spikes, safety stock floors, overtime constraints, and changeover costs — all unified in a single MIP formulation.

🎯
Zero backorders achieved at 95% CSL target. Setup cost accounts for only 10% of total cost — demonstrating efficient batching via Silver-Meal warm start.
